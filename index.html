About the Company

Home Link Textiles is built around quality production, practical pricing, and dependable delivery

The company presents itself as a quality-oriented textile house focused on export manufacturing and large-scale supply. It aims to provide buyers with competitive prices, consistent output, and a complete sourcing solution under one roof.

What the Company Does

Home Link Textiles manufactures and exports home furnishing textiles, hotel and hospital linen, made-ups, bedding products, and garments. It has particular strength in bed spreads, pillow protectors, comforters, and filling-based textile products.

  • Export-oriented textile manufacturing house based in Pakistan.
  • Serves local and international buyers with customized and standard products.
  • Focuses on price competitiveness, quality, and service reliability.

Mission & Buyer Promise

To provide textile buyers a complete and dependable supply solution that combines product range, manufacturing capability, inspection control, and efficient execution.
